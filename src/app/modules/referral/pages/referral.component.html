How are referral payments calculated?

Once a Referred Creator has become a registered User of Chello in accordance with the rules of the Chello Referral Program described above, the Referring User will be paid a referral payment equal to two per cent (2%) of Fan Payments generated by the Referred Creator. There is a 24 months time limit on the referral terms, which begins when the referred Creator pays their first admin fee.

How frequently are referral payments made?

The referral payment due to you on Fan Payments made to the Referred Creator in a calendar month will be transferred to you on the 3rd day of the next calendar month (which means, for example, that referral payments due to you in respect of Fan Payments made to the Referred Creator in April shall be paid to you on or around May 3rd).

Who bears the cost of the referral payment?

The cost of the referral payment is borne by us, not the Referral Creator.
