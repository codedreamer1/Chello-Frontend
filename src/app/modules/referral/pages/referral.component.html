Yes, there is a referral program with Chello. Every Creator will be given a referral code. In order to qualify for the referral program, the Creator has to pay the admin fee of $25 per month or $240 annual. The Creator will be paid a 3% referral revenue every month on the 3rd, for the Creators they referred to Chello. The 3% is from the referred Creator business for the previous month. Referral payments:

Every Creator will be given a referral code. In order to qualify for the referral program, the Creator has to pay the admin fee of $25 per month or $240 annual. The Creator will be paid a 3% referral revenue every month on the 3rd, for the Creators they referred to Chello. The 3% is from the referred Creator business for the previous month. This is one half of the 6% Chello receives. There is no time limit on the referrals.

For example, Creator A refers to Creators B and C to Chello. In March, Creator B made $6000 and Creator C made $4000. On April 3, Creator A will have credited $300 referral revenue.

How are referral payments calculated?

Once a Referred Creator has become a registered User of Chello in accordance with the rules of the Chello Referral Program described above, the Referring User will be paid a referral payment equal to three per cent (3%) of Fan Payments generated by the Referred Creator. There is no maximum or time limit on the referral terms.

How frequently are referral payments made?

The referral payment due to you on Fan Payments made to the Referred Creator in a calendar month will be transferred to you on the 3rd day of the next calendar month (which means, for example, that referral payments due to you in respect of Fan Payments made to the Referred Creator in April shall be paid to you on or around May 3rd).

Who bears the cost of the referral payment?

The cost of the referral payment is borne by us, not the Referral Creator.
